MINI FILLS

Mini fills are meant to be a “pick me up” for your lashes

Meaning, you MUST have 75% retention to book a mini fill. Mini fills are usually booked a week or so after your regular lash fill.

I personally use mini fills for last minute events that I didn’t plan for when scheduling my two week fills.

Mini fill are NOT meant to take place of your regular two week fill. Please book responsibly so that I don’t have to result in taking mini fills off my menu

Policies

Clean Lashes

Please arrive to your appointment with fresh, clean lashes! If lashes are dirty & require cleaning, an additional $15.00 will be added on the final service price.

Arrival & Additional Guests

Please arrive on time & alone. No family, friends, or children. Take this time to relax!

Non-refundable Deposit

All full sets require a $50.00 non-refundable deposit to be received within 24 hours of booking your appointment or it will be canceled.
This $50.00 will go towards the full service price.

Payment & Refunds

Cash & Zelle are the ONLY forms of payment accepted. Please be prepared for your appointment.
Refunds WILL NOT be issued. Please understand that when a service is completed, time & product have been used. If you experience more fall-out than usual please reach out within TWO DAYS.

No Call No Show

No call & no show will NOT be tolerated.
You will NOT be allowed to reschedule.
You WILL be charged the full service amount.
